## Vacancy Posting Checklist (Per Unit)

When a unit becomes available, post to ALL of these:

- [ ] AppFolio listing published (syndicates to Zillow, Apartments.com, etc.)
- [ ] Facebook Marketplace
- [ ] Craigslist ([city] section)
- [ ] Facebook Page post
- [ ] 2-3 Facebook local housing groups
- [ ] Google Business Profile post
- [ ] Nextdoor (if claimed)

Refresh/renew every 7 days until leased.
