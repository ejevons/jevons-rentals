Living in Our Rental Communities

Discover what makes each area a great place to rent a home.

Renting in Yakima

Yakima offers some of the most affordable rental housing in Washington State. With a lower cost of living than the Puget Sound, renters enjoy spacious apartments and houses at prices well below the state average. Close to wineries, outdoor recreation, and a growing downtown scene.

Affordable rent Low cost of living Wine country Outdoor recreation Family friendly

Renting in Tacoma & Lakewood

Pierce County rentals put you close to JBLM, the Port of Tacoma, and easy I-5 access to Seattle. Tacoma's revitalized downtown, Museum District, and waterfront make it one of the best rental markets in the South Sound. Lakewood and Puyallup offer suburban living with urban convenience.

Near JBLM I-5 corridor Waterfront Museums & culture Transit access

Renting in Seattle, Kent & Federal Way

King County offers access to the state's largest job market, world-class dining, and iconic Pacific Northwest scenery. Our rentals in Kent and Federal Way provide affordable alternatives to downtown Seattle while keeping you connected via light rail and major highways.

Major employers Light rail access Diverse dining Parks & trails Airport proximity

Renting in Moses Lake & Ephrata

Grant County offers the most affordable rentals in our portfolio. Moses Lake is a growing community with excellent schools, lakeside recreation, and a tight-knit community feel. Ideal for families looking for space and value in Central Washington.

Most affordable Lake recreation Growing community Good schools Small-town feel

Apartments for Rent in Yakima, WA

Our Yakima County rentals include studio, one-bedroom, two-bedroom, and three-bedroom apartments in Yakima, Selah, and Union Gap. Yakima is one of the most affordable rental markets in Washington State, making it ideal for first-time renters, families, and anyone looking for a lower cost of living without sacrificing quality.

Houses & Apartments for Rent in Tacoma, Lakewood & Puyallup

Pierce County is one of our fastest-growing rental markets. We offer apartments, townhomes, duplexes, and single-family houses for rent near Joint Base Lewis-McChord (JBLM), downtown Tacoma, and suburban Puyallup. Our Lakewood rentals are especially popular with military families and commuters.

Rentals in Seattle, Kent & Federal Way

King County renters benefit from proximity to Seattle's job market, light rail transit, Sea-Tac Airport, and world-class amenities. Our rental homes in Kent and Federal Way offer more affordable alternatives to downtown Seattle while keeping you connected to everything the region has to offer.

Affordable Rentals in Moses Lake & Ephrata

Grant County features our most affordable rental housing. Moses Lake rentals are perfect for families looking for space, lake recreation, strong schools, and a small-town atmosphere in Central Washington. Our Ephrata apartments offer similar value in a quiet, close-knit community.
